Moshood Temitope is a Front-End Engineer, User Interface Designer & User Experience researcher based in Lagos, Nigeria. I am passionate about developing interative designs for web applications. I also design user interfaces for web applications. I conduct User Experience Researches about how people use products.
I currently work at VConnect. My focus is on the Business Owners Dashboard product. The Business Owners product is a flagship product of the organization as thousands of Business Owners manage their business enquiries and customers with this product.
Prior to VConnect, I worked at Orbi Technologies as a Front End Engineer where I designed the entire interface for the version 2.0 of the Orbi product, a web application for a social media product based on voice notes. I also briefly led the Windows Mobile team working on the mobile version of the this flagship product.
Outside of work, I voluntarily mentor web developers(a recent activity being a mentor at Andela Learning Community ) and write articles about my learnings on my Blog.
